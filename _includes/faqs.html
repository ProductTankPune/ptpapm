Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Product Tank Pune APM program cost anything?

The Product Tank Pune APM program is completely free for both the candidates as well as the participating companies. Product Tank Pune does not charge any fees for the program either from the candidates or from the companies. It is an initiative to open up more opportunities for PM candidates.

Is this a full time program ?

Yes, this is a full time program for a duration of 6 months.

Will I be paid during the program ?

Yes, you will get a salary as decided by the company you get selected for during the APM program.

Is this similar to an internship?

This is very much similar to an internship but instead of just working on internship projects , you will be operating as a PM in full capacity. You will also receive weekly online mentorship and guidance throughout the duration of the program.

Is it guaranteed that I will be hired by the company post completion of the APM program?

The APM program does not guarantee that the company will hire you after the completion of the APM program. However, the guidance and the mentorship you receive from the mentors throughout the duration of the program will help you perform better on the job and increase the likelihood of you getting hired post the completion of the program.

What kind of candidates are you looking for?

The requirements for each company may vary.Across the board, we are looking for self-driven and motivated candidates who are passionate about Product Management. Some of the requirements of an ideal candidate are:

  • Prior Work experience with the ability to deliver results while learning on the job.
  • An understanding of how software is built and delivered.
  • Strong interpersonal and stakeholder management skills.
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Insights about good digital products.
  • Eye for detail and a knack for solving problems.
  • Experience of working in a team is desired but you are equally comfortable being a solo contributor.

How can my company get involved?

Companies willing to be a part of the program can reach out to us at producttankpune@gmail.com. We'd encourage product leads from your company to reach out to know more about how your company can get involved and help us create the next generation of product managers and a thriving product management industry in India.
