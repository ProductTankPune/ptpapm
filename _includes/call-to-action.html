Breaking into Product Management the right way !

Product Tank Pune's APM program aims at creating the next generation of Product leaders in India by taking a small group of self-driven candidates who are enthusiastic about building products through a training path involving mentorship, on-the-job training and providing them a valuable network that will prepare them for future product leadership.

As an APM, you will:

  • Work closely with PMs to develop end-to-end features that address real user needs.
  • Collaborate with engineers, UX designers, marketing, etc to launch your features.
  • Collaborate with senior PMs to work on the roadmap prioritization.
  • Get access to weekly guidance sessions from industry's finest product managers.
